About Cell-Phenotype


Cell-Phenotype is a online community that explore the cell-phenotype associations.

The cell-phenotype associations were generated by Expression Weighted Celltype Enrichment (EWCE) using single-cell transcriptomics data from Tabula Muris that were clustered and annotated into 38 cell types and genotype-to-phenotype annotations from MGI.

8353 MPO phenotypes were performed for the EWCE enrichment tests and 14787 significant cell-phenotype associations were obtained.

The researchers can retrieve the association results by searching for cell type or phenotypes and explore the cross-species phenotypes between MPO and HPO.

The code used in this cell-phenotype association study is available at Github.
